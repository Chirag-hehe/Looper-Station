Privacy

Local-first by design.

Looper Station is built so recordings and saved takes stay in your browser unless you choose to export them.

Data handled

The app can request microphone access, MIDI access, and local browser storage. Audio is processed in the browser for live looping. Saved takes are stored locally with IndexedDB on the device and browser you use.

Data not collected

This local build does not send recordings, MIDI messages, saved sessions, or microphone audio to a server. Exported WAV files are created in your browser and downloaded by you.

Permissions

Microphone permission is needed to record audio. MIDI permission is optional and only needed for external controllers. Browser storage is used for saved takes.

Future hosting

If this project is hosted online or released as an Android app, this policy should be updated with the final domain, support contact, analytics choices, crash reporting choices, and any cloud features.
